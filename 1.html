Physics Formulas

Mechanics

Speed: \( \text{Speed} = \frac{\text{Distance}}{\text{Time}} \)

Velocity: \( v = \frac{\text{Displacement}}{\text{Time}} \)

Acceleration: \( a = \frac{\text{Change in Velocity}}{\text{Time}} = \frac{\Delta v}{\Delta t} \)

First Equation of Motion: \( v = u + at \)

Second Equation of Motion: \( s = ut + \frac{1}{2}at^2 \)

Third Equation of Motion: \( v^2 = u^2 + 2as \)

Force: \( F = ma \)

Weight: \( W = mg \)

Momentum: \( p = mv \)

Newton's Second Law (Momentum Form): \( F = \frac{\Delta p}{\Delta t} \)

Impulse: \( J = F \Delta t = \Delta p \)

Work, Power, and Energy

Work: \( W = F \cdot s \cdot \cos\theta \)

Power: \( P = \frac{W}{t} \)

Kinetic Energy: \( KE = \frac{1}{2}mv^2 \)

Potential Energy: \( PE = mgh \)

Law of Conservation of Energy: \( KE + PE = \text{Constant} \)

Mechanical Advantage: \( MA = \frac{\text{Load}}{\text{Effort}} \)

Efficiency: \( \eta = \frac{\text{Useful Work Output}}{\text{Work Input}} \times 100\% \)

Electricity

Ohm's Law: \( V = IR \)

Electric Power: \( P = VI \)

Resistance in Series: \( R_{\text{total}} = R_1 + R_2 + \dots \)

Resistance in Parallel: \( \frac{1}{R_{\text{total}}} = \frac{1}{R_1} + \frac{1}{R_2} + \dots \)

Electric Energy: \( E = P \cdot t \)

Joule's Law of Heating: \( H = I^2 R t \)

Electric Power (Alternate Forms): \( P = I^2 R \), \( P = \frac{V^2}{R} \)

Current: \( I = \frac{Q}{t} \)

Electromotive Force (EMF): \( \epsilon = V + Ir \)

Optics

Mirror Formula: \( \frac{1}{f} = \frac{1}{u} + \frac{1}{v} \)

Lens Formula: \( \frac{1}{f} = \frac{1}{v} - \frac{1}{u} \)

Magnification: \( m = \frac{h'}{h} = \frac{v}{u} \)

Power of Lens: \( P = \frac{1}{f} \) (f in meters)

Refractive Index: \( n = \frac{c}{v} \), where \( c \) is speed of light in vacuum, \( v \) is speed in medium

Snell's Law: \( n_1 \sin \theta_1 = n_2 \sin \theta_2 \)

Lens Maker's Formula: \( \frac{1}{f} = (n-1) \left( \frac{1}{R_1} - \frac{1}{R_2} \right) \)

Apparent Depth: \( d' = \frac{d}{n} \)

Gravitation

Universal Law of Gravitation: \( F = G \frac{m_1 m_2}{r^2} \)

Acceleration due to Gravity: \( g = \frac{G M}{R^2} \)

Gravitational Potential Energy: \( U = -\frac{G m_1 m_2}{r} \)

Escape Velocity: \( v_e = \sqrt{\frac{2GM}{R}} \)

Orbital Velocity: \( v_o = \sqrt{\frac{GM}{r}} \)

Magnetism

Magnetic Field due to a Straight Conductor: \( B = \frac{\mu_0 I}{2 \pi r} \)

Magnetic Field at Center of Circular Loop: \( B = \frac{\mu_0 I}{2 r} \)

Force on a Current-Carrying Conductor: \( F = B I L \sin\theta \)

Force between Two Parallel Conductors: \( F = \frac{\mu_0 I_1 I_2}{2 \pi d} \cdot L \)

Magnetic Field Inside Solenoid: \( B = \mu_0 n I \)

Sound

Speed of Sound in a Medium: \( v = \sqrt{\frac{B}{\rho}} \), where \( B \) is bulk modulus, \( \rho \) is density

Frequency of Wave: \( f = \frac{1}{T} \), where \( T \) is time period

Wavelength: \( \lambda = \frac{v}{f} \)

Intensity of Sound: \( I = \frac{P}{A} \), where \( P \) is power, \( A \) is area

Speed of Sound in Air: \( v = 331 + 0.6 T \) (T in °C)

Echo Time: \( t = \frac{2d}{v} \)

Pressure

Pressure: \( P = \frac{F}{A} \)

Atmospheric Pressure: \( P = h \rho g \), where \( h \) is height of column, \( \rho \) is density

Buoyant Force: \( F_b = \rho V g \), where \( V \) is volume of displaced fluid

Archimedes' Principle: Buoyant force equals weight of displaced fluid

Pascal's Law: Pressure transmitted equally in all directions

Hydraulic Lift: \( F_2 = F_1 \frac{A_2}{A_1} \)

Heat

Heat Capacity: \( Q = m c \Delta T \)

Latent Heat: \( Q = m L \)

Thermal Expansion (Linear): \( \Delta L = L \alpha \Delta T \)

Thermal Expansion (Area): \( \Delta A = A 2\alpha \Delta T \)

Thermal Expansion (Volume): \( \Delta V = V 3\alpha \Delta T \)

Mathematics Formulas

Algebra

Quadratic Formula: For \( ax^2 + bx + c = 0 \), roots are \( x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a} \)

Arithmetic Progression (nth term): \( a_n = a + (n-1)d \)

Sum of n terms of AP: \( S_n = \frac{n}{2} [2a + (n-1)d] \)

Sum of n terms (alternate): \( S_n = \frac{n}{2} (a + l) \), where \( l \) is the last term

Geometric Progression (nth term): \( a_n = a r^{n-1} \)

Sum of n terms of GP: \( S_n = a \frac{1 - r^n}{1 - r} \) (for \( r \neq 1 \))

Sum of infinite GP: \( S_\infty = \frac{a}{1 - r} \) (for \( |r| < 1 \))

Sum of Squares of First n Natural Numbers: \( \sum n^2 = \frac{n(n+1)(2n+1)}{6} \)

Sum of Cubes of First n Natural Numbers: \( \sum n^3 = \left( \frac{n(n+1)}{2} \right)^2 \)

Polynomial Remainder Theorem: If \( p(x) \) is divided by \( (x - a) \), remainder is \( p(a) \)

Factor Theorem: If \( p(a) = 0 \), then \( (x - a) \) is a factor of \( p(x) \)

Geometry

Area of Triangle: \( A = \frac{1}{2} \cdot \text{base} \cdot \text{height} \)

Area of Circle: \( A = \pi r^2 \)

Circumference of Circle: \( C = 2\pi r \)

Area of Rectangle: \( A = \text{length} \cdot \text{width} \)

Area of Square: \( A = \text{side}^2 \)

Pythagoras Theorem: \( a^2 + b^2 = c^2 \)

Area of Parallelogram: \( A = \text{base} \cdot \text{height} \)

Area of Trapezium: \( A = \frac{1}{2} \cdot (\text{sum of parallel sides}) \cdot \text{height} \)

Area of Rhombus: \( A = \frac{1}{2} \cdot d_1 \cdot d_2 \), where \( d_1, d_2 \) are diagonals

Perimeter of Rhombus: \( P = 4 \cdot \text{side} \)

Heron's Formula for Triangle Area: \( A = \sqrt{s(s-a)(s-b)(s-c)} \), where \( s = \frac{a+b+c}{2} \)

Trigonometry

Basic Ratios:

\( \sin \theta = \frac{\text{Opposite}}{\text{Hypotenuse}} \)

\( \cos \theta = \frac{\text{Adjacent}}{\text{Hypotenuse}} \)

\( \tan \theta = \frac{\text{Opposite}}{\text{Adjacent}} \)

Trigonometric Identities:

\( \sin^2 \theta + \cos^2 \theta = 1 \)

\( 1 + \tan^2 \theta = \sec^2 \theta \)

\( 1 + \cot^2 \theta = \csc^2 \theta \)

Additional Identities:

\( \sin(A + B) = \sin A \cos B + \cos A \sin B \)

\( \cos(A + B) = \cos A \cos B - \sin A \sin B \)

\( \tan(A + B) = \frac{\tan A + \tan B}{1 - \tan A \tan B} \)

Values for Common Angles:

\( \sin 0^\circ = 0, \sin 30^\circ = \frac{1}{2}, \sin 45^\circ = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}, \sin 60^\circ = \frac{\sqrt{3}}{2}, \sin 90^\circ = 1 \)

\( \cos 0^\circ = 1, \cos 30^\circ = \frac{\sqrt{3}}{2}, \cos 45^\circ = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}, \cos 60^\circ = \frac{1}{2}, \cos 90^\circ = 0 \)

\( \tan 0^\circ = 0, \tan 30^\circ = \frac{1}{\sqrt{3}}, \tan 45^\circ = 1, \tan 60^\circ = \sqrt{3}, \tan 90^\circ = \text{undefined} \)

Complementary Angles: \( \sin(90^\circ - \theta) = \cos \theta \), \( \cos(90^\circ - \theta) = \sin \theta \)

Mensuration

Surface Area of Cuboid: \( 2(lb + bh + hl) \)

Volume of Cuboid: \( l \cdot b \cdot h \)

Surface Area of Cube: \( 6a^2 \)

Volume of Cube: \( a^3 \)

Surface Area of Cylinder: \( 2\pi r (r + h) \)

Volume of Cylinder: \( \pi r^2 h \)

Volume of Cone: \( \frac{1}{3} \pi r^2 h \)

Surface Area of Sphere: \( 4\pi r^2 \)

Volume of Sphere: \( \frac{4}{3} \pi r^3 \)

Curved Surface Area of Cone: \( \pi r l \), where \( l = \sqrt{r^2 + h^2} \)

Total Surface Area of Cone: \( \pi r (r + l) \)

Volume of Hemisphere: \( \frac{2}{3} \pi r^3 \)

Curved Surface Area of Hemisphere: \( 2\pi r^2 \)

Total Surface Area of Hemisphere: \( 3\pi r^2 \)

Volume of Frustum of Cone: \( \frac{1}{3} \pi h (r_1^2 + r_2^2 + r_1 r_2) \)

Coordinate Geometry

Distance Formula: \( \sqrt{(x_2 - x_1)^2 + (y_2 - y_1)^2} \)

Section Formula: For ratio \( m:n \), coordinates are \( \left( \frac{m x_2 + n x_1}{m + n}, \frac{m y_2 + n y_1}{m + n} \right) \)

Midpoint Formula: \( \left( \frac{x_1 + x_2}{2}, \frac{y_1 + y_2}{2} \right) \)

Slope of a Line: \( m = \frac{y_2 - y_1}{x_2 - x_1} \)

Equation of a Line (Slope-Intercept Form): \( y = mx + c \)

Equation of a Line (Point-Slope Form): \( y - y_1 = m (x - x_1) \)

Area of Triangle (Coordinate Geometry): \( A = \frac{1}{2} | x_1(y_2 - y_3) + x_2(y_3 - y_1) + x_3(y_1 - y_2) | \)

Centroid of Triangle: \( \left( \frac{x_1 + x_2 + x_3}{3}, \frac{y_1 + y_2 + y_3}{3} \right) \)

Statistics

Mean (Arithmetic Mean): \( \bar{x} = \frac{\sum x_i}{n} \)

Median (for odd n): \( \text{Median} = \left( \frac{n+1}{2} \right)^{\text{th}} \text{ term} \)

Median (for even n): \( \text{Median} = \frac{\left( \frac{n}{2} \right)^{\text{th}} \text{ term} + \left( \frac{n}{2} + 1 \right)^{\text{th}} \text{ term}}{2} \)

Mode: The value that appears most frequently in the data

Mean Deviation about Mean: \( MD = \frac{\sum |x_i - \bar{x}|}{n} \)

Mean Deviation about Median: \( MD = \frac{\sum |x_i - \text{Median}|}{n} \)

Empirical Relation: Mode = 3 Median - 2 Mean

Variance: \( \sigma^2 = \frac{\sum (x_i - \bar{x})^2}{n} \)

Standard Deviation: \( \sigma = \sqrt{\frac{\sum (x_i - \bar{x})^2}{n}} \)

Probability

Probability of an Event: \( P(E) = \frac{\text{Number of favorable outcomes}}{\text{Total number of outcomes}} \)

Complementary Events: \( P(\text{not } E) = 1 - P(E) \)

Probability of Mutually Exclusive Events: \( P(A \text{ or } B) = P(A) + P(B) \)

Probability of Independent Events: \( P(A \text{ and } B) = P(A) \cdot P(B) \)

Conditional Probability: \( P(A|B) = \frac{P(A \cap B)}{P(B)} \)

Circles

Equation of Circle: \( (x - h)^2 + (y - k)^2 = r^2 \)

Length of Tangent from External Point: \( L = \sqrt{(x_1 - h)^2 + (y_1 - k)^2 - r^2} \)

Angle between Two Radii and Chord: Equal in the same segment

Alternate Segment Theorem: Angle in alternate segment equals angle subtended by chord

Area of Sector: \( \frac{\theta}{360^\circ} \pi r^2 \)

Length of Arc: \( \frac{\theta}{360^\circ} 2\pi r \)
